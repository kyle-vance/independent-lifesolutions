Independent Life Solutions was founded by Trevor Arave and Kyle Vance, two medical professionals and life-long friends in northern Utah. Both Trevor and Kyle are licensed medical professionals who have spent the majority of their careers providing health care for an aging population. One fated day, a patient expressed their frustration of waiting for over 2 months to have their home safety equipment installed. A need was identified and they got to work. Independent Life Solutions was built from the ground up to help fill this void. Real medical professionals to provide in-home safety evaluations
